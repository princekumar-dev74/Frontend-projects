In Cinemas Soon

無限城の戦い

The Final Confrontation

Infinity Castle plunges Tanjiro, Nezuko, the Hashira, and the Demon Slayer Corps into Muzan’s otherworldly fortress—a shifting labyrinth of peril. Inside, they face the Upper Rank demons in intense, one-on-one battles that set the stage for the saga’s ultimate confrontation.

The film premiered in Japan on July 18, 2025, and shattered records—achieving the biggest opening day and single-day earnings in Japanese history, with over ¥7.31 billion ($49.5 million) in just four days. Within two weeks, it crossed ¥15 billion ($102 million), becoming the fastest to reach that milestone. By day 22, it climbed to around ¥20.3 billion (~$138 million), ranking as the 7th highest-grossing film ever in Japan.

A global rollout is underway: the film reached Taiwan and Southeast Asian markets starting August 8, with releases in North America, India, the UK, and Canada set for September 12, 2025—including IMAX formats.

Produced by:Akifumi Fujio, Masanori Miyake, Yūma Takahashi(Animated by: ufotable)

The elite Hashira swordsmen and main characters who take on Muzan Kibutsuji's forces

Demon Slayer Hashira

Giyu Tomioka

水の呼吸

Japanese Voice

Takahiro Sakurai

The Water Hashira. A stoic but compassionate swordsman who was the first Hashira to recognize Tanjiro's potential. His Breath of Water techniques are among the most versatile.

Kyojuro Rengoku

炎の呼吸

Japanese Voice

Satoshi Hino

The Flame Hashira. An enthusiastic and honorable swordsman who serves as a role model for younger Demon Slayers. His powerful Flame Breathing techniques generate intense fire and heat.

Tengen Uzui

音の呼吸

Japanese Voice

Katsuyuki Konishi

The Sound Hashira. A flashy former shinobi with enhanced hearing abilities. His Sound Breathing allows him to detect enemies by their "sound" and anticipates attacks before they happen.

Shinobu Kocho

昆虫の呼吸

Japanese Voice

Saori Hayami

The Insect Hashira. Kind and graceful, but deadly. Unable to decapitate demons, she uses Insect Breathing and a poison-laced blade to deliver swift, precise strikes that target demons’ vital weaknesses.

Mitsuri Kanroji

恋の呼吸

Japanese Voice

Kana Hanazawa

The Love Hashira. Cheerful and warm-hearted with unmatched physical strength. Her Love Breathing blends flexibility and speed, wielding a whip-like blade capable of unpredictable, flowing strikes that overwhelm even the fastest demons.

Gyomei Himejima

石の呼吸

Japanese Voice

Tomokazu Sugita

The stone Hashira. Gentle and devout, yet the Corps’ strongest warrior. Blind but incredibly perceptive, his Stone Breathing combines immense strength and a massive weapon to crush even the toughest demons.

Muichiro Tokito

ミストブリージング

Japanese Voice

Kengo Kawanishi

The Mist Hashira. A young prodigy with exceptional reflexes and focus. His Mist Breathing creates deceptive movements and illusions, overwhelming opponents before they can react, despite his calm and detached demeanor.

Obanai Iguro

蛇の呼吸

Japanese Voice

Kenichi Suzumura

The serpent Hashira. Strict and disciplined, deeply loyal to the Corps. His Serpent Breathing uses twisting, unpredictable sword paths, mirroring a snake’s movement to bypass defenses and strike at precise angles.

Sanemi Shinazugawa

風の呼吸

Japanese Voice

Kenichi Suzumura

The Wind Hashira. Hot-tempered yet fiercely protective, scarred from relentless battle. His Wind Breathing delivers relentless, slicing attacks from multiple angles, designed to keep opponents constantly off-balance and unable to counterattack.

Team Tanjiro

Tanjiro Kamado

日の呼吸

Japanese Voice

A kind-hearted and determined boy whose family was slaughtered by demons, leaving his sister as the only survivor. He wields the Breath of Water and later develops the Breath of the Sun, fighting tirelessly to find a cure for Nezuko.
